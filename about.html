The term Late Capitalism been widely used since the 21th century around the world to refer to perceived absurdities, contradictions, crises, injustices, and inequality created by modern business development.
Humansoflatecapitalism.com is a website dedicated to spreading awareness and educating people about the phenomenon of "late capitalism." Our goal is to capture the aesthetics of this period in history and provide a platform for like-minded individuals to come together and organize for change.

Our Organize page offers a chatroom where people with a shared interest in overcoming late capitalism can connect and collaborate. Meanwhile, our Top100 feature allows users to vote for their favorite #latecapitalism memes, while our Blog features occasional articles exploring the issues surrounding late capitalism.

Through our website, we hope to inspire a greater understanding of late capitalism and empower individuals to take action against it. Whether you're an artist, activist, or concerned citizen, Humansoflatecapitalism.com is the perfect place to connect with others who share your passion for change.
